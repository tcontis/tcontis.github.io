Wire-Cutting Machine

Motivation

For our ECE 4180 (Embedded Systems Design) final project, two teammates and I decided to help HKN automate its wire-production process for lab kits. Every semester, as part of a lab-packaging event, members cut and strip over 800 wires, which represents a lot of time and effort.
